# Social Media Do’s and Don’ts :-

##  Do’s
- Think before you post anything online and consider how it might affect you in the future  
- Use respectful and polite language while interacting with others  
- Keep your personal information safe, such as your phone number, address, and passwords  
- Check if information is true before sharing it with others  
- Maintain a positive and professional online presence  
- Use privacy settings to control who can see your posts  
- Take part in meaningful and respectful discussions  
- Give proper credit when sharing someone else’s work or ideas  

##  Don’ts
- Don’t share sensitive personal details publicly  
- Don’t post offensive, harmful, or inappropriate content  
- Don’t spread fake news or unverified information  
- Don’t get involved in unnecessary online arguments or negativity  
- Don’t use abusive or unprofessional language  
- Don’t post content that could harm your reputation in the future  
- Don’t copy or repost content without giving credit  
- Don’t overshare personal life details online
