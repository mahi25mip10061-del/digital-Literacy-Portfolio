# Task 4: Professional Email & Digital Etiquette

## Overview
This task focuses on understanding and applying professional communication skills in a digital environment. It includes writing formal emails and creating guidelines for responsible social media use.

---

## Part A: Professional Emails
In this section, two formal emails were drafted:

- **Email 1:** A request to a professor asking for an extension on an assignment deadline.
- **Email 2:** An email expressing interest in a summer internship opportunity.

Both emails follow proper email etiquette, including:
- Clear subject lines  
- Professional greetings  
- Well-structured content  
- Polite and respectful tone  
- Proper sign-off  

---

## Part B: Social Media Checklist
A checklist was created to highlight responsible social media behavior for college students. It includes:

- **Do’s:** Best practices for maintaining a positive and safe online presence  
- **Don’ts:** Common mistakes to avoid while using social media  

This helps in promoting digital responsibility and awareness among students.
